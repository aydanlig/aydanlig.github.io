Support me for free

There are lots of ways to help that do not require money. If you enjoy my work, your attention and participation can make a real difference.

  • React to my posts, share them, and help them reach more people.
  • Make appreciation content such as art reactions, fan edits, or short videos.
  • Send me files, references, or source materials so I can upload and edit them.
  • Help me find artistic partnerships, creative collaborators, or events to attend.
  • Bring me into spaces where people can see my work and connect with me in person.

My goal is to create more smiles, build meaningful artistic partnerships, and show up at events or networking spaces where people can discover my work.

If you want to help in a more direct way, email aydan@smileyfoxyportraits.com.
